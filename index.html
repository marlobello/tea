How to get your interval data

Sign in to your Smart Meter Texas account and open Export Energy Usage Data, then:

  1. Set Report Type to Energy Data 15 Min Interval.
  2. Set the Start date to just under 24 months ago (the End date can stay as today).
  3. Click Submit Update.
  4. Click Export My Report to download your CSV — then upload that file above.
